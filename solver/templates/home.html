How to use this Website to solve wordle

  1. In the given below form enter your word
  2. Now you have to enter color code associated with the respective letters
  3. Color codes are -> B for black , G for green and Y for yellow

    Example
    1. For example your word is aegis
    2. And color associated with respective letters in wordle is :
      1. a : green that is a is at correct position
      2. e : black that is e is not present in the correct answer
      3. i : black that is i is not present in the correct answer
      4. g : black that is g is not present in the correct answe
      5. s : yellow that is s is present in correct answer but at some other position
    3. So your color code to enter into form will be -> GBBBY (in any case)
